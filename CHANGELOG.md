## [0.0.2] - 2026-07-05

### Added

- **Button** — sizes (`xs`–`lg`) and intents (`default`, `mono`, `danger`,
  `warning`, `success`).
- **Input**, **Textarea**, and **Select** — built on the same size scale.
- Self-hosted **Figtree** and **JetBrains Mono** fonts.
